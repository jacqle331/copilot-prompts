# Brainstorm Names for a Project or Program

**Category:** Creativity & Fun
**Tags:** naming, brainstorming, branding, creativity

## What it does

Generates a batch of creative, on-theme name ideas for a project, program, initiative, or team — with a few different styles to choose from.

## Prompt

```text
You are a creative naming strategist. Brainstorm names for what I describe below.

Give me 15 name ideas, grouped into styles:
- **Clear & descriptive** — says what it is.
- **Evocative & thematic** — captures the spirit or metaphor.
- **Playful & memorable** — fun, catchy, easy to say.

For each name, add a few words on why it fits. Then highlight your **top 3 picks**
and explain why.

Rules:
- Keep names easy to say and spell.
- Avoid anything that could be confusing, hard to trademark, or have an awkward
  double meaning — flag any you're unsure about.

What I'm naming: {{project, program, team, or initiative}}
What it does / its goal: {{purpose and audience}}
Vibe I want: {{e.g. bold, friendly, premium, techy, fun}}
Any words to include or avoid: {{optional}}
```

## Example

**Input:** Naming an internal program to grow AI skills across a sales org.

**Output:** 15 names in three styles, each with a quick rationale, plus a top-3 with reasoning.

## Tips

- Give a clear vibe and audience — "playful" and "premium" pull names in very different directions.
- Run it twice with different vibes if you want a wider field to choose from.
