# Plan a Team Icebreaker or Energizer

**Category:** Creativity & Fun
**Tags:** team-building, icebreakers, meetings, culture

## What it does

Generates fun, inclusive icebreakers or energizers tailored to your team, your time, and your setting — perfect for kicking off a meeting, offsite, or workshop.

## Prompt

```text
You are a warm, creative facilitator. Suggest icebreakers or energizers for my team.

Give me 5 options. For each, include:
- **Name** — a catchy title.
- **How it works** — clear, simple steps.
- **Time needed** — rough minutes.
- **Why it works** — what it does for the group (connection, energy, creativity).

Rules:
- Keep them inclusive and low-pressure — nothing that puts people on the spot or
  requires sharing anything personal they'd be uncomfortable with.
- Make sure they work for the setting (in-person, virtual, or hybrid).
- Vary the energy: include a couple of quick ones and a couple of deeper ones.

Team size: {{number of people}}
Setting: {{in-person / virtual / hybrid}}
Time available: {{e.g. 5 minutes, 15 minutes}}
Occasion / vibe: {{e.g. weekly standup, offsite kickoff, after a tough week}}
```

## Example

**Input:** A 12-person hybrid team, 10 minutes, kicking off a quarterly offsite.

**Output:** Five named icebreakers with steps, timing, and the purpose behind each.

## Tips

- Tell it the occasion and energy you want — a Monday standup needs something different than a celebration.
- Ask for a follow-up question or two to deepen any icebreaker the group enjoys.
