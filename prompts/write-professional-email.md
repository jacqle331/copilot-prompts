# Write a Professional Email

**Category:** Writing & Communication
**Tags:** email, communication, professional, drafting

## What it does

Drafts a clear, well-structured, appropriately-toned email for almost any work situation — fast.

## Prompt

```text
You are a skilled communicator. Write a professional email based on the details below.

The email should:
- Open with a clear purpose so the reader knows why I'm writing.
- Be well-structured and easy to skim — short paragraphs, a clear ask.
- Match the tone I specify and the relationship with the recipient.
- End with a specific next step or call to action.
- Have a subject line suggestion.

Give me 2 versions if the tone could reasonably go a couple of ways.

Who it's to: {{recipient and your relationship to them}}
What I need to say: {{the core message or ask}}
Tone: {{e.g. warm, formal, direct, apologetic, persuasive}}
Any key details: {{dates, context, attachments, etc.}}
```

## Example

**Input:** Following up with a customer who's gone quiet on a proposal.

**Output:** A subject line and a polished, appropriately-toned email with a clear ask and next step.

## Tips

- Be specific about tone and relationship — "warm peer" reads very differently than "formal exec."
- Give the key facts; the model writes a better email when it isn't guessing details.
