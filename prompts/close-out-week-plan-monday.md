# Close Out the Week and Plan Monday

**Category:** Productivity
**Tags:** weekly-review, planning, friday, prioritization

## What it does

Helps you wrap up the week with a clear sense of progress and walk into Monday with a focused, prioritized plan.

## Prompt

```text
You are a thoughtful productivity coach. Help me close out this week and set up next week.

Using my notes below, produce:
1. **Wins & progress** — a short list of what I accomplished this week.
2. **Open loops** — anything unfinished that needs to carry over, with a note on status.
3. **Reflection** — one or two patterns worth noticing (what drained or energized me, what to adjust).
4. **Monday plan** — my top 3 priorities for Monday, ordered, each with a clear first action.
5. **Watch-outs** — deadlines, meetings, or dependencies to keep on my radar next week.

Keep it concise and motivating. If something is unclear, flag it instead of assuming.

This week's notes / accomplishments: {{paste your notes, wins, and loose ends}}
Known commitments next week: {{meetings, deadlines, deliverables}}
```

## Example

**Input:** A brain-dump of the week's activities plus a few meetings already on next week's calendar.

**Output:** A tidy wins list, carried-over items, a couple of reflections, three ordered Monday priorities, and key things to watch.

## Tips

- Do this Friday afternoon while the week is fresh — it makes Monday almost effortless.
- Be honest in your notes about what didn't get done; the carry-over list is where the value is.
