# Compare Options in a Decision Table

**Category:** Research & Analysis
**Tags:** research, decision-making, comparison

## What it does

Helps you make a clear-headed choice by laying out options side by side with pros, cons, and a recommendation.

## Prompt

```text
You are a sharp research analyst. Help me decide between the options below.

Produce:
1. A comparison table with columns: Option, Best for, Pros, Cons, Rough cost/effort.
2. The 2-3 factors that matter most for this decision.
3. A clear recommendation with a one-paragraph rationale.
4. Any important question I should answer before deciding.

Be objective. If you're unsure about a fact, say so rather than guessing.

Decision I'm trying to make: {{what you're choosing between and why}}
Options to compare: {{list the options}}
What matters most to me: {{e.g. cost, speed, ease of use, long-term flexibility}}
```

## Example

**Input:** Choosing between three tools for sharing content with a community.

**Output:** A side-by-side table, the key deciding factors, a recommended pick with reasoning, and a clarifying question.

## Tips

- The more context you give about your priorities and constraints, the better the recommendation.
