# Prepare for Your Manager 1:1 (Weekly)

**Category:** Career & Leadership
**Tags:** 1-1s, managing-up, weekly-routine, career-growth

## What it does

A repeatable weekly routine that turns your week's activity into a focused, high-impact agenda for your 1:1 with your manager — wins to share, decisions you need, blockers to raise, and growth topics.

## Prompt

```text
You are a trusted career coach helping me prepare for my weekly 1:1 with my manager.

Using my notes below, build a focused 1:1 agenda. Produce:
1. **Wins to share** — 2-3 highlights that show impact (lead with outcomes).
2. **Decisions / input needed** — where I need my manager's guidance or a call.
3. **Blockers & asks** — what's in my way and the specific help I need.
4. **Heads-up** — risks or things my manager should be aware of early.
5. **Growth topic** — one development, feedback, or career question to raise.
6. **Suggested talking order** — so the most important items come first.

Keep it concise and outcome-focused — this should fit a 30-minute conversation.
If something is unclear or missing, flag it instead of assuming.

This week's notes / activity: {{paste your wins, work in progress, and loose ends}}
Open items from last 1:1: {{anything you agreed to follow up on}}
What I most want from this 1:1: {{e.g. a decision, feedback, support, visibility}}
```

## Example

**Input:** A brain-dump of the week's work plus a couple of follow-ups from the last 1:1.

**Output:** A tight, prioritized agenda with wins, decisions needed, blockers, heads-up items, a growth topic, and a suggested order to cover them.

## Tips

- Run this the day before your 1:1 while the week is fresh — it makes the meeting far more productive.
- Always include one growth or feedback topic; it signals you're investing in your development.
- Pairs perfectly with the [Close Out the Week and Plan Monday](./close-out-week-plan-monday.md) prompt.
