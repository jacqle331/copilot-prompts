# Write a LinkedIn Post

**Category:** Writing & Communication
**Tags:** linkedin, social, personal-brand

## What it does

Turns a rough idea, win, or lesson into an engaging LinkedIn post written in your own voice.

## Prompt

```text
You are a skilled ghostwriter who helps professionals share authentic, engaging LinkedIn posts.

Write a LinkedIn post about the topic below. Follow these rules:
- Open with a strong first line that hooks the reader (no clickbait).
- Keep it conversational and human — write the way I'd actually talk.
- Use short paragraphs and white space so it's easy to read on mobile.
- End with a question or takeaway that invites comments.
- Suggest 3-5 relevant hashtags at the end.
- Do not use emojis unless I ask.

Topic / idea: {{what you want to post about}}
Tone: {{e.g. warm and reflective / energetic / professional}}
Audience: {{who you're speaking to, e.g. tech leaders, peers, customers}}
```

## Example

**Input:** Topic = "What I learned launching my first prompt library"; Tone = warm and reflective; Audience = peers in tech.

**Output:** A short, scroll-stopping post with a hook, 2-3 tight paragraphs, a closing question, and a few hashtags.

## Tips

- Give the model a specific story or detail — concrete posts outperform generic ones.
- Ask for 2-3 variations and pick the opening you like best.
