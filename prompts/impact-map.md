# Create an Impact Map (Visual Career Map)

**Category:** Career & Leadership
**Tags:** career-growth, impact, visualization, image-generation, personal-brand

## What it does

Generates a clean, whiteboard-style one-page "Impact Map" that visualizes your work life — what you do, who you work with, your values, what drives you, and your growth path over time — with your own headshot blended naturally into the design. A powerful personal leadership map, not a resume.

## Prompt

```text
Create a photorealistic image in a clean whiteboard sketch style that visualizes my work life.

The image should clearly show:
1) What I do — my core responsibilities and where I create impact
2) Who I work with — customers, teammates, partners, stakeholders
3) My role — my title and how I connect to the bigger picture
4) My values — how I work and what I stand for
5) What's important to me — what drives me and what I care about
6) My growth path — a clear progression of how I evolve my impact over time

Design guidance:
- Whiteboard sketch aesthetic: hand-drawn lines, simple icons, labeled boxes and arrows
- Clean, organized, easy to scan like a thoughtful one-page career map
- Minimal accent colors on a white background
- Make the growth path visually prominent (left → right progression)

Photo:
- Use my uploaded headshot (photorealistic, NOT cartoon)
- Blend the photo naturally into the design (soft edges, slight sketch overlay, subtle shadow)
- Avoid a cutout look — it should feel integrated into the whiteboard scene

About me (EDIT THIS SECTION):
<Replace with your own description of your work, impact, values, and what matters to you.
You can paste a short paragraph or upload your resume to guide the content.>

Growth path (EDIT THIS SECTION):
- TODAY: <what you drive today — execution + impact>
- NEXT: <how you expand into strategy, broader influence, AI, or scale>
- FUTURE: <how you lead at scale — growth, transformation, developing others>

Tone:
- Clear, human, and non-generic
- Focus on impact and how I work (not just responsibilities)
- Feels like a personal leadership map, not a resume

Additional input:
- Resume: optional upload for more context
```

## Example

**Input:** Your uploaded headshot, a short paragraph about your work and values, and a filled-in TODAY → NEXT → FUTURE growth path.

**Output:** A one-page whiteboard-style Impact Map with your photo blended in, your work life mapped out, and a prominent left-to-right growth path.

## Tips

- **Upload a clear, front-facing headshot** — it blends far more naturally into the design.
- **Fill in the two EDIT sections** with real, specific detail (or paste your resume) — generic input produces a generic map.
- Frame the growth path around *impact and how you work*, not just titles — that's what makes it a leadership map, not a resume.
- Re-run it yearly or after a big role change to see how your impact evolves.
