# Write a Team Celebration or Shout-Out

**Category:** Creativity & Fun
**Tags:** recognition, culture, celebration, team-morale

## What it does

Helps you write a warm, specific, and genuine message to celebrate a win or recognize someone's contribution — without sounding generic or corporate.

## Prompt

```text
You are a thoughtful leader who's great at making people feel genuinely appreciated.

Write a celebration or shout-out message based on the details below. It should:
- Be specific about what the person or team did and the impact it had.
- Sound warm and human — not generic or corporate.
- Match the channel and tone I specify.
- Be the right length for where it's going (short for chat, a bit fuller for email).

Give me 2 versions so I can pick the one that fits best.

Who I'm recognizing: {{person or team}}
What they did: {{the specific accomplishment or behavior}}
The impact: {{why it mattered — the result, or who it helped}}
Where it's going: {{e.g. Teams channel, email, all-hands, LinkedIn}}
Tone: {{e.g. heartfelt, fun and upbeat, professional}}
```

## Example

**Input:** Recognizing a teammate who pulled off a tough customer save under deadline.

**Output:** Two warm, specific shout-out messages sized for your chosen channel.

## Tips

- Name the concrete impact — "saved the renewal" lands far harder than "great work."
- Public recognition means more when it's specific; give the model real details.
